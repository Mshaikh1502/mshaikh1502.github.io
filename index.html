Fuel efficiency is important not only for your wallet but also for combating climate change. This graph is designed to explore the fuel efficiency of various cars.

Gasoline Observations: Note how gasoline cars tend to offer a much higher Highway to City MPG ratio. There is a direct correlation between MPG on the highway and in the city though.

Diesel Observations: Note how diesel cars tend to offer similar MPG across the board, but they still sport that high Highway to City MPG ratio.

Electricity Observations: Note how electric cars, although they don't use gas, still have a MPG calculation (this is actually MPGe). Effectively they use less energy and go farther. Oddly enough their Highway to City MPG ratio is much lower than other vehicles, making them perfect for normal drivers.
